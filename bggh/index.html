Boston Graffiti Ghost Hunt is a collective art restoration project. The history of graffiti in Boston dates back to the ealry 80’s yet there is no memorandum for those accomplished artist who made the graffiti scene in Boston what it is today. Lets help revitalize their spirits and educate new writers to respect influential pieces which still stand. BGGH started as a timeline installation which told the story of Boston’s place in American graffiti from the introduction of Wild Style in 83 to the interconnection of street art by Dan Plasma in 1994. Stickers were placed on empty walls where pieces once stood, telling the story of the artist and crew. We indicate the location of each sticker with this icon:

Now we want you to continue this story. Upload pictures of old pieces and share with us any information that you can without sabotaging the artist. All user generated entries are indicated by this circle icon.
