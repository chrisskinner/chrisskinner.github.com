Guide for New Bostonians

2009

The 2009 class, working with the Mayor’s Office of New Bostonians, designed a guidebook for new immigrants to the city of Boston. Topics included housing, education, banking, transportation, social customs, and healthcare. (Translated editions were produced in 2011)
