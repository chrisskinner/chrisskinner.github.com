Homeless: a Resource Guide for Students and Teachers

2004

The 2004 class collaborated with staff from the Boston Public Schools to design and edit a magazine-style textbook to teach middle- and high-school students about the issue of family homelessness. The book was distributed to students in the Boston Public School system, and later was picked up by school systems in several towns outside Boston as well.
