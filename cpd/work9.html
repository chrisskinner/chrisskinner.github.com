The Halftone Book

2002

The 2001 class (our very first) designed this book for graphic designers to understand how halftoning works in the printed reproduction of photographic images. The book opened from two directions, with the same content printed on coated and uncoated paper for direct comparison, and the two halves separated by a glossary of terms.
