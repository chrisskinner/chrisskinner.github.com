about

Extreme Moderation is a personal fitness counselling service.

The first step is to aid you in discovering the obstacle(s). Then the fun begins. We offer you uniquely creative tools to help you make the changes you want to make at a pace that is comfortable for you.

We don’t give you an exercise routine. We help you discover what exercise routine out there or created by you works for you. For one person it might be walking the dog two blocks further than the day before. Another person might have to overcome a perceived judgment that if he or she exercises, it should be at the level it was before children were born.Each one of us has challenges to taking care of our bodies. The Extreme Moderation approach helps the individual take small steps to overcome those challenges in the safety of a confidential consultation via skype, facetime, phone, or in person.

Patty Aicher currently lives in Montclair New Jersey. After 25 years of personal training experience, Patty took a seven year break to discover and establish the principles and core methods of Extreme Moderation.
