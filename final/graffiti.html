Boston Graffiti Ghost Hunt

Installation for the history of graffiti in Boston. Dating back to 1983, This scavenger hunt guides the viewer through areas in boston where influential artworks once existed.

Criteria: Create an installation which acts as a timeline for a historical event in Boston.
