WHCD Cocktail Reception

Yahoo News and ABC News were asked to set up the Cocktail Reception before the White House Correspondents' Dinner. Working with both sets of assets I created a design system that gave Yahoo and ABC a 50/50 split of brand presence. Furniture and print material were selected to emulate old-school White House furniture and decoration.
