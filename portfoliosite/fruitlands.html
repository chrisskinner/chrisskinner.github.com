Fruitlands Museum is an outdoor art and history museum located in
Harvard Massachusetts. The museum consists of four houses scattered
across the land each holding their own subject matter relative to the
area: the Alcott Transcendentalist Farmhouse, the Shaker Museum, the
Native American Art Museum and the Hudson River School Paintings.
I decided to brand the museum as a whole as well as each house.
