Steve Skinner’s studio is built in the sunroom of his home in Montclair, NJ. The room has excellent acoustics and provides a bright, sunny work environment. It has two listening orientations, one optimized for arranging and recording, the other for accurate mixing. He has developed and built a unique system of recording electric guitars that allows the artist to quickly try various hardware amps and settings before or after the part has been recorded.
